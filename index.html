Welcome to SamSonRepas

Experience the authentic taste of African cuisine blended with Canadian favorites in the heart of Gatineau, QC. Our founder Christopher Samson brings years of experience from working at Burger King and has traveled extensively across Africa to master traditional dishes. With a wife of African descent inspiring our flavors, we proudly serve a menu that celebrates rich heritage and fresh local ingredients.

Chef's Story

Christopher Samson is originally from Quebec. As a young man, he worked at Burger King, where he developed a passion for food service. Driven by curiosity and a love for flavors, he traveled extensively across the African continent, immersing himself in the diverse culinary traditions. Inspired and guided by his wife, who is of African descent, Christopher mastered authentic African cuisine recipes. His unique blend of African and Canadian culinary knowledge is what makes SamSonRepas a one-of-a-kind dining experience.
